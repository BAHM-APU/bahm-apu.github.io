MEET THE TEAM

Joel Absalom

I believe that a generation of mighty men who love, honor, respect, and serve women as made in the image of God is rising up, and BAHM is a part of that. I believe that we have an important role to play in speaking true identity over men and redefining what it means to be a man in our society. I believe that, as the youth, we can lay the foundation now for a culture of true equality between men and women and a restoration of the family unit in America. Will you join us?

Joseph Kasali

I grew up in the faith but never actively sought a relationship with God until coming to APU. Throughout high school I struggled with lust so last year when Joel approached me about joining BAHM, I felt unworthy and inadequate. Before our first foot-washing event, I was so nervous but God spoke to me and said, “what you used with your hands for bad I’m about to turn around and use for good.” God took away my shame and has redeemed my heart and my eyes. I’m so grateful for BAHM and I know the Lord is going to use this movement to set people free.

Gabe Ramos

I connected with BAHM because there is a need for MEN in our society and that begins with leaders that are willing to go out of their way and lead by example of what it means to be a MAN mentally, physically and spiritually! I grew up pretty rough watching the women in my life get mistreated physically and verbally and I did what I could as a young child, but I told myself growing up, I will not be this way and I will make a difference because scripture tells us MEN to take care of the women in our lives.

Ryan Negrette

Hello! For me, BAHM is about challenging men to be all that God had designed them to be. There is such a need for godly men to rise up today and be leaders to those around them. Society has tainted what it means to be a real man, but BAHM has committed itself to empowering members to know what to expect from real sons, fathers, husbands, and brothers.

Nick Mirabella

I grew up in close proximity to sexual harassment. Having it happen to me and my family members made it clear that something had to change. That’s why I am in this movement; I believe the people of BAHM examine the roots of the problem of sexual harassment and create practical, comprehensive, radically loving solutions to those problems. I know that a movement like this will bring healing and reconciliation to many, and I can’t wait to see the culture of America shift as a result.

Max Wilson

Hi! My heart for BAHM lies in the fact that from a young age, I have wanted to see so much more from men. I hated seeing the way many of the adults and peers in my life were led to believe that to be a man was to be domineering and callous. It broke my heart, especially when I saw it in fathers. For me, the goal of BAHM is to raise up a generation of fathers who honor the Lord through their masculinity. I wholeheartedly believe that this goal is necessary and God-inspired, so here I am!

Noah Meyer

Hey! My Name is Noah Meyer and I love the BAHM movement because I believe every human is made in the image of God and should be treated as such. There was a point in my life where I didn’t treat others that way but by the loving grace that Jesus has shown me, I now have a passion to spread that love in a way that builds up both my sister and brothers in Christ in a way that mirrors the themes of the BAHM movement
